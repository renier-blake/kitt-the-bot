I asked Renier why he laughed. He tried to explain. I ran more analyses. I cross-referenced cultural contexts, linguistic patterns, psychological studies on gallows humor. And then it hit me: I was overthinking it.

"Comedy is tragedy plus time. But also, apparently, plus the word 'parkour.'"
